Course Facilities

Melbourne Meadows is a premier golf course located just a chip shot away from Lake Saratoga in Saratoga Springs, New York. We offer a large driving green, perfect for getting your swing right for 18 holes of golf. You can also get some needed practice on our state of the art chipping and putting greens.

Course Details

Melbourne Meadows is an 18 hole PGA-level golf course featuring some of the best views of Saratoga Lake available. Designer Michael Robinson used the natural landscape to create beautifully crafted holes surrounded by northeast pines and rolling brooks. The course was constructed and is maintained to meet PGA Tour standards at a public course price.

Weddings

Let our professional catering and experienced wedding planning team take care of all of your wedding needs. We offer a variety of different outdoor wedding locations along the lake and streams that surround our beautiful course, as well as an indoor venue that is great for any occasion
